What data do we collect?

We collect only minimal data required for the app to function:

  • Texts you create inside the app.
  • Words you save to your personal vocabulary list.
  • Your email address (used for account login and identification).
  • If you sign in with Google, we collect the following personal information from your Google account:
    • Your Name and Profile Picture: To personalize your experience and display it in your user profile.
    • Your Email Address: To uniquely identify your account for login and communication purposes.

We do not collect your phone number, IP address, or location data.

How do we collect your data?

We collect data only when you actively use the app:

  • When you generate and save texts.
  • When you tap to save vocabulary words.
  • When you sign up or log in to your account.

All data collection is limited to your interaction within the app. No data is collected in the background.

How will we use your data?

We use your data only to provide and improve the core features of the app:

  • To store and show you the texts and words you've saved.
  • To create your user account and manage your session.
  • To generate personalized content using a third-party AI service provider.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or user profiling.

Do we share your data?

Yes, but only in these specific technical cases to make the app work:

  • The text prompts you enter are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate content on demand. We do not share any personal identification data (like your name or email) with this service.
  • If you purchase a subscription, purchase-related data is securely processed by our subscription provider (e.g., Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

Your data is never sold or shared for advertising purposes.

How do we store your data?

We store your data securely and responsibly:

  • All data is stored using Supabase infrastructure.
  • Your data is linked to your user account and is kept as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently erased from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

Your data protection rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your saved data.
  • Request to correct or delete your data from our systems.
  • Contact us for any privacy concern.

To exercise these rights, please email us at: readyoursapp@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected a child's personal data, we will take steps to delete that information.

Legal basis for data processing

We process your personal data based on your consent, or when it is necessary to deliver the services offered in the app (our contractual obligation to you). We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Changes to our privacy policy

We regularly review this policy. Any updates will be posted in the app and on this page.
